About Me

My name is Carter Watson. I'm from northern Utah but plan to relocate to Southern California in early 2024. I'll graduate from Utah State University in Dec 2023 with my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science.

I have two years of part time experience working as a software engineer in the defense industry. In that time I've learned a lot, mostly about coding and best practices and workflows and the like.

Outside of work I enjoy cycling, video games, and traveling! I am currently training for my first century (100mi) ride. I am a huge Nintendo fan and enjoy playing their classics on my GameCube and their modern games on my Switch. My most recent trip was down to Sedona, Arizona, where I saw the Chapel of the Holy Cross and Devil's Bridge.
